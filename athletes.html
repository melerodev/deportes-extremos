Featured Athletes

Alex Thompson

Skateboarding

Age: 26

From: Los Angeles, USA

Alex Thompson is one of the most innovative street skaters of his generation. Known for his technical precision and creativity, he has redefined what's possible in modern skateboarding. Starting at the age of 7, Alex quickly rose through the amateur ranks before turning pro at 18.

Career Highlights:

  • Three-time X Games gold medalist (2019, 2021, 2022)
  • Street League Skateboarding World Champion (2020)
  • Featured in 'Evolution of Skate' documentary series
  • Developed signature trick: 'The Thompson Flip'

Sophia Rodriguez

Snowboarding

Age: 24

From: Denver, Colorado

Sophia Rodriguez has dominated women's halfpipe snowboarding since her breakthrough performance at the Winter Olympics. Her amplitude, technical precision, and innovation have set new standards in the sport. Born into a family of winter sports enthusiasts, Sophia was on a snowboard by age 4.

Career Highlights:

  • Olympic Gold Medalist in Halfpipe (2022)
  • Two-time X Games Gold Medalist (2020, 2023)
  • World Snowboarding Championship winner (2021)
  • First woman to land a frontside double cork 1080 in competition

Marcus Wilson

BMX

Age: 28

From: Austin, Texas

Marcus Wilson is a pioneering BMX rider who has pushed the boundaries of what's possible on two wheels. His fearless approach to big air competitions and technical street riding has earned him legendary status. Marcus grew up riding dirt jumps he built in his backyard and competing in local competitions.

Career Highlights:

  • Five-time X Games medalist (3 gold, 1 silver, 1 bronze)
  • Nora Cup Street Rider of the Year (2019, 2021)
  • Red Bull Dirt Conquers champion (2020)
  • Creator of the 'Wilson 540' trick variation

Kai Nakamura

Surfing

Age: 23

From: Oahu, Hawaii

Kai Nakamura has quickly become one of surfing's most exciting talents, known for taking on the biggest waves with unmatched style and confidence. Growing up on the North Shore of Oahu, Kai was immersed in surf culture from birth and caught his first wave at age 5, mentored by local legends.

Career Highlights:

  • World Surf League Championship Tour winner (2022)
  • Pipeline Masters champion (2021)
  • Big Wave Award for Ride of the Year (2023)
  • Featured in 'Ocean Warriors' documentary series
